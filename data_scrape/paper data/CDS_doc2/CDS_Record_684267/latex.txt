**Summary of ATLAS System Milestones**

This document lists ATLAS "LHCC" milestones for the initial detector configuration, with their latest Baseline and Actual dates. The schedule has been rebaselined after the new LHC schedule (start-up April 2007). Following this exercise, the Baseline date is the Achieved date for past milestones and the Expected date for future milestones. The Ready for Installation (RFI) milestones are given for each system. These are defined by the Installation Schedule with a four month float. A few systems do not at present expect to meet these RFI milestones. In these cases the "Actual" date is the expected date agreed after ATLAS internal review.
